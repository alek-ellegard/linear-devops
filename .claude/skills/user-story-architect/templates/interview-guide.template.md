## Core Discovery Questions

### Current State Understanding

1. **Workflow Mapping**
   - Walk me through your typical workflow when you need to [task]
   - How often do you do this? (daily/weekly/monthly)
   - How long does it typically take?
   - What tools do you use?

2. **Dependencies**
   - What happens before you can start?
   - Who provides input to you?
   - Who uses your output?
   - How do you know if it succeeded?

3. **Pain Points**
   - What's the most frustrating part?
   - What takes the most time?
   - What errors commonly occur?
   - Can you give me a specific example of when this broke?

### Desired State Exploration

4. **Priority Improvements**
   - If you could fix one thing, what would it be?
   - Why is this the most important?
   - What would change if this was fixed?
   - Who else would benefit?

5. **Success Criteria**
   - How would you measure success?
   - What metrics matter to you?
   - What absolutely cannot change?
   - What would break if we got this wrong?

### Technical Deep Dives

6. **Integration Points**
   - What other systems does this connect to?
   - What data formats are used?
   - What are the performance requirements?
   - What security constraints exist?

7. **Edge Cases**
   - What happens when [component] fails?
   - How do you handle [unusual scenario]?
   - What's your rollback process?
   - How do you handle peak load?

8. **Constraints and Requirements**
   - What compliance requirements exist?
   - What are the hard technical limits?
   - What legacy systems must we work with?
   - What migration concerns exist?

## Validation Questions

9. **Understanding Check**
   - Let me verify: you're saying [summary]?
   - The main problem is [X] and solving it means [Y]?
   - Did I miss anything critical?

10. **Prioritization**
    - On a scale of 1-10, how critical is [feature]?
    - If we could only do three things, what would they be?
    - What's the minimum viable solution?
